Make

“Make” activities are hands-on tutorials for building and creating the physical objects that accompany many of the creative computing projects you’ll find on this site. Examples of “Make” activities include: building paper lanterns, constructing a miniature greenhouse, starting plant seedlings, and building circuits using electronics.
