Learning Ecosystems and Family Science (LEaFS)

LEaFS is a pilot project designed to engage the whole family in the design, construction, and maintenance of a technology-enhanced hydroponic food growing system at home. Families will learn how computer science concepts and plant biology concepts can be combined to create a food growing system that is easy to maintain and fun to experiment with.
