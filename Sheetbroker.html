Sheetbroker : A Tool for Logging Data to Google Sheets

Sheetbroker is a tool developed by the Laboratory for Playful Computation to make it easier to connect student-friendly programmable microcontrollers like the micro:bit to google sheets. Unlike other data logging tools, this method can live stream data straight to google sheets without needing to keep the data collection device plugged into the computer. Then students can analyze and visualize their collected data in real-time.
